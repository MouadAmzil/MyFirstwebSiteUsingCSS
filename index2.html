Sri Lanka's Best Beach Resort

Calamander Unawatuna Beachis the only four star resort hotel located directly on Sri Lanka's best beach, Unawatuna, on which it has the beach frontage of 135 meters.

THE BEACH

Unawatuna beach,next to Galle on the island’s south coast, is rated one of the best beaches in the world and is famous for its natural beauty and intimacy. Unawatuna was voted‘The World’s Best Tropical Beach’by The Discovery Channel, and‘Best Beach in the World’by Mark Ellingham, founder of the Rough Guides. No other beach resort in Sri Lanka offers the same combination of golden sand, a protected bay, spectacular sunsets, Sri Lanka’s safest swimming all year round, and a reputation for being hassle free and laid back; all conveniently close to a wide variety of shopping, dining and cultural attractions only 15 minutes away in Galle Fort, Sri Lanka’s Riviera and a UNESCO heritage site. This combination of attractions, unique in Sri Lanka, is just 90 minutes away from Colombo airport. Paradise has never been so accessible

THE RESORT

Calamander Unawatuna Beach is an 'affordable luxury',family friendly resort that celebrates its superb location.The resort is all about being outdoors:sun bathing on one of the world's best beaches,joining a game of beach volleyball or a yoga class,lounging by one of Sri lanka's three best swimming pools, kayaking on the Indian Ocean in one of our free kayaks, or dining al-fresco at one of our four beachside restaurants and bars.Surrounded by lush greenery, the luxury Chalets in the hills provides the privacy you desire for your holiday getaway. The perfect stay for Golfers to tee off at the picture Perfect Nuwara Eliya, Golf Club.

THE BEACH

Great design is part of the calamander experience.Our objective is to provide world-class beach location.The highlight of our design is our pools and our restaurant.These include the world's first vermillion coloured swimming pool,Sri lanka's first glass fronted pool,and a restaurant designed as a contemporary interpretation of the ancient 'Magul Maduwa', The Royal Audience Hall in Kandy,which has the longest solid timber roof built in Sri Lanka this century.The highlight of our design is our pools and our restaurant. Our Sun Pool is the world’s first vermillion coloured swimming pool, the colour of the Unawatuna sunset, and shimmers with beautiful Italian mosaic tiles. The turtles that lay their eggs on Unawatuna Beach inspired our children’s pool, The Turtle Pool, which is inlaid with a glittering mosaic turtle. Finally,The Blu Pool, Sri Lanka’s first glass fronted pool (and Asia Pacific’s longest) is one of the few pools in the world to sit directly on a famous beach, from which you get a beautiful view of the Indian Ocean.
